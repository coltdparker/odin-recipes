Pizza

Home

Italian dish featuring a round, flattened dough base topped with tomato sauce, cheese (typically mozzarella), and various ingredients like meats, vegetables, and herbs, then baked at high temperature. Originated in Naples, now exists in countless regional and global variations with different crusts, sauces, and toppings.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Pasta sauce
  • Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Spread out the dough into a circle by spinning it on your hand
  2. Put sauce on top
  3. Cover with the parmesan cheese
  4. Cook in oven for 18 minutes
