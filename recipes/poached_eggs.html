Poached Eggs

Home

Eggs cooked without their shell in simmering water, resulting in firm whites and runny yolks. Often served on toast, in eggs Benedict, or atop salads and grain bowls.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions

  1. Stir water heated in pan
  2. Pour egg into the center of the vortex from the shell
  3. Remove with a strainer when cookec
  4. Salt, pepper, and serve!
